Nurturing Creativity in Early Childhood Education

Experts,and professional educators found several skills and abilities which the infant's need in the present and also in the future (Trilling & Fadel 2009, 8-11; AMA 2010,3). children need to be
  • Creative thinkers which makes the child able to deal with practical and abstract issues, some of which have not even arisen.
  • Flexible and be able to adjust in an increasingly complex world filled with various of information, technology,and diverse people with lot's of competitors.
  • Problem solvers who will be ready for the challenges and opportunities they will face.
